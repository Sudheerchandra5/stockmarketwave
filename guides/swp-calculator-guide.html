SWP Calculator Guide: Plan Mutual Fund Withdrawals

A systematic withdrawal plan can help create regular cash flow from a mutual fund corpus, but the withdrawal amount and return assumption matter.

What SWP calculates

An SWP calculator estimates how much corpus may remain after regular withdrawals and assumed investment growth over time.

Key inputs to review

Starting corpus, monthly withdrawal, expected return, and duration are the main inputs. Taxes, exit load, and market volatility should also be considered.

How to use SWP results

If the projected corpus falls too quickly, lower the withdrawal amount, shorten the withdrawal period, or use a more conservative return assumption.
