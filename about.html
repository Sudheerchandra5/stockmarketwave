About StockMarketWave

StockMarketWave is an educational calculator hub built to help investors and learners understand common stock market, mutual fund, and ETF calculations.

What we provide

We provide manual-input calculators for profit/loss, SIP, SWP, STP, ETF returns, dividends, taxes, valuation ratios, portfolio allocation, and risk analysis. Each calculator is designed to be simple, transparent, and formula-backed.

Our approach

The site starts with manual inputs so the calculators can work without paid market data APIs. Users can enter values from brokers, fund statements, exchanges, or research sources and review the formula behind the result.

Educational purpose

StockMarketWave is not a brokerage, advisor, or financial recommendation service. The tools are intended for education, planning, and learning how common investment calculations work.
