When to use this calculator

Use this XIRR calculator approximation for investments with irregular cash flows, such as multiple SIPs, redemptions, switches, or lump-sum additions.

How to interpret the result

XIRR annualizes returns when cash flows happen at different times. It is generally more suitable than simple CAGR for irregular mutual fund transactions.

Common mistakes to avoid

Use accurate transaction dates and cash-flow amounts for precise XIRR. This calculator provides a planning estimate and should be verified for formal reporting.
