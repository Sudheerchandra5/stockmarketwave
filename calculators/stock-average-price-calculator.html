When to use this calculator

Use this when you buy the same stock multiple times at different prices and want the weighted average cost. It helps investors understand their true cost basis after averaging up or averaging down.

How to interpret the result

The average price is the total invested amount divided by total shares. If charges are included, the average price reflects a more realistic cost per share.

Common mistakes to avoid

Do not use a simple average of prices when purchase quantities differ. Weighted average price gives the correct result because each lot may have a different number of shares.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is the Stock Average Price Calculator used for?

The Stock Average Price Calculator is a free stocks calculator that works out average price from lot 1 shares, lot 1 price, lot 2 shares, lot 2 price, lot 3 shares, lot 3 price, and total charges / fees. Enter your own figures to get an instant result with the formula shown on the page.

What formula does the Stock Average Price Calculator use?

It calculates Total shares = Shares 1 + Shares 2 + Shares 3, Total cost = Sum of (shares x price) + charges, and Average price = Total cost / Total shares. The tool reports average price, total shares, total cost, and charges included.

Where do I get the numbers for the Stock Average Price Calculator?

Take lot 1 shares, lot 1 price, lot 2 shares, lot 2 price, lot 3 shares, lot 3 price, and total charges / fees from your broker statement or exchange. The Stock Average Price Calculator never fetches live market data, so the output depends only on the values you type in.

Are the Stock Average Price Calculator results financial advice?

No. The Stock Average Price Calculator is an educational tool. Treat average price as an estimate, re-check your inputs, and confirm important investment decisions with a qualified professional.

Can I use the Stock Average Price Calculator with any currency or market?

Yes. Keep lot 1 shares, lot 1 price, lot 2 shares, lot 2 price, lot 3 shares, lot 3 price, and total charges / fees in the same currency and consistent units, and the Stock Average Price Calculator applies the same calculation to any market.
