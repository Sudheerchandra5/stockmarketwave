What this calculator helps with

The Risk Reward Ratio Calculator estimates risk per share from entry price, stop-loss price, and target price. It is a focused stock calculator you can use free with no signup, repeating the calculation with your own numbers whenever your inputs change.

How to read the results

After you press Calculate result, the summary cards show risk per share, reward per share, reward / Risk, and break-even win rate. Read risk per share as the headline figure and use the supporting values for context, then change one input at a time to see how sensitive the result is.

Before relying on the numbers

This stock calculator applies the formula Entry price - Stop-loss price. Enter entry price, stop-loss price, and target price using consistent units and the same currency, include any applicable fees or taxes, and confirm important decisions against your broker, fund statement, exchange data, or a qualified professional.
