FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is the Cash Secured Put Calculator used for?

The Cash Secured Put Calculator is a free stocks calculator that works out cash required from put strike price, premium received, contracts, and contract multiplier. Enter your own figures to get an instant result with the formula shown on the page.

What formula does the Cash Secured Put Calculator use?

It calculates Cash required = (Strike - Premium) x Contracts x Multiplier and Max profit = Premium x Contracts x Multiplier. The tool reports cash required, max profit, break-even stock price, and return on cash.

Where do I get the numbers for the Cash Secured Put Calculator?

Take put strike price, premium received, contracts, and contract multiplier from your broker statement or exchange. The Cash Secured Put Calculator never fetches live market data, so the output depends only on the values you type in.

Are the Cash Secured Put Calculator results financial advice?

No. The Cash Secured Put Calculator is an educational tool. Treat cash required as an estimate, re-check your inputs, and confirm important investment decisions with a qualified professional.

Can I use the Cash Secured Put Calculator with any currency or market?

Yes. Keep put strike price, premium received, contracts, and contract multiplier in the same currency and consistent units, and the Cash Secured Put Calculator applies the same calculation to any market.
