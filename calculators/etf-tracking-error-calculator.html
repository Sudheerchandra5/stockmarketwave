The ETF Tracking Error Calculator is a free etfs calculator that works out tracking difference from etf return (%), benchmark return (%), and number of periods. Enter your own figures to get an instant result with the formula shown on the page.

What formula does the ETF Tracking Error Calculator use?

It calculates Tracking difference = ETF return - Benchmark return. The tool reports tracking difference, average monthly gap, etf return, and benchmark return.

Where do I get the numbers for the ETF Tracking Error Calculator?

Take etf return (%), benchmark return (%), and number of periods from your ETF provider page or exchange. The ETF Tracking Error Calculator never fetches live market data, so the output depends only on the values you type in.

No. The ETF Tracking Error Calculator is an educational tool. Treat tracking difference as an estimate, re-check your inputs, and confirm important investment decisions with a qualified professional.

Can I use the ETF Tracking Error Calculator with any currency or market?

Yes. Keep etf return (%), benchmark return (%), and number of periods in the same currency and consistent units, and the ETF Tracking Error Calculator applies the same calculation to any market.
