Frequently asked questions

What is the Pledge Margin Calculator used for?

The Pledge Margin Calculator is a free stocks calculator that works out usable margin from holding value and haircut (%). Enter your own figures to get an instant result with the formula shown on the page.

What formula does the Pledge Margin Calculator use?

It calculates Usable margin = Holding value x (1 - Haircut %) and Haircut amount = Holding value x Haircut %. The tool reports usable margin, haircut amount, margin percentage, and holding value.

Where do I get the numbers for the Pledge Margin Calculator?

Take holding value and haircut (%) from your broker statement or exchange. The Pledge Margin Calculator never fetches live market data, so the output depends only on the values you type in.

Are the Pledge Margin Calculator results financial advice?

No. The Pledge Margin Calculator is an educational tool. Treat usable margin as an estimate, re-check your inputs, and confirm important investment decisions with a qualified professional.

Can I use the Pledge Margin Calculator with any currency or market?

Yes. Keep holding value and haircut (%) in the same currency and consistent units, and the Pledge Margin Calculator applies the same calculation to any market.
