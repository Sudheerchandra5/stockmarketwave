FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is the Futures Profit Calculator used for?

The Futures Profit Calculator is a free stocks calculator that works out futures profit / loss from entry price, exit price, contracts, and contract multiplier. Enter your own figures to get an instant result with the formula shown on the page.

What formula does the Futures Profit Calculator use?

It calculates Futures P/L = (Exit price - Entry price) x Contracts x Multiplier. The tool reports futures profit / loss, price move, contract exposure, and return on exposure.

Where do I get the numbers for the Futures Profit Calculator?

Take entry price, exit price, contracts, and contract multiplier from your broker statement or exchange. The Futures Profit Calculator never fetches live market data, so the output depends only on the values you type in.

Are the Futures Profit Calculator results financial advice?

No. The Futures Profit Calculator is an educational tool. Treat futures profit / loss as an estimate, re-check your inputs, and confirm important investment decisions with a qualified professional.

Can I use the Futures Profit Calculator with any currency or market?

Yes. Keep entry price, exit price, contracts, and contract multiplier in the same currency and consistent units, and the Futures Profit Calculator applies the same calculation to any market.
