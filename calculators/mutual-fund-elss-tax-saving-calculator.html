Frequently asked questions

What is the ELSS Tax Saving Calculator used for?

The ELSS Tax Saving Calculator is a free mutual funds calculator that works out tax saving from elss investment, tax slab (%), expected return (%), and years. Enter your own figures to get an instant result with the formula shown on the page.

What formula does the ELSS Tax Saving Calculator use?

It calculates Tax saving = Investment x Tax slab and Future value = Investment x (1 + Return) ^ Years. The tool reports tax saving, future value, estimated gain, and lock-in years.

Where do I get the numbers for the ELSS Tax Saving Calculator?

Take elss investment, tax slab (%), expected return (%), and years from your fund statement or scheme factsheet. The ELSS Tax Saving Calculator never fetches live market data, so the output depends only on the values you type in.

Are the ELSS Tax Saving Calculator results financial advice?

No. The ELSS Tax Saving Calculator is an educational tool. Treat tax saving as an estimate, re-check your inputs, and confirm important investment decisions with a qualified professional.

Can I use the ELSS Tax Saving Calculator with any currency or market?

Yes. Keep elss investment, tax slab (%), expected return (%), and years in the same currency and consistent units, and the ELSS Tax Saving Calculator applies the same calculation to any market.
