What this calculator helps with

The Trailing Stop Loss Calculator estimates trailing stop price from current price, trailing stop (%), and shares. It is a focused stock calculator you can use free with no signup, repeating the calculation with your own numbers whenever your inputs change.

How to read the results

After you press Calculate result, the summary cards show trailing stop price, trail distance, protected value, and current position value. Read trailing stop price as the headline figure and use the supporting values for context, then change one input at a time to see how sensitive the result is.

Enter current price, trailing stop (%), and shares using consistent units and the same currency, include any applicable fees or taxes, and confirm important decisions against your broker, fund statement, exchange data, or a qualified professional.
