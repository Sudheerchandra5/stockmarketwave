FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is the Fund Comparison Calculator used for?

The Fund Comparison Calculator is a free mutual funds calculator that works out fund a value from investment amount, fund a return (%), fund a expense (%), fund b return (%), fund b expense (%), and years. Enter your own figures to get an instant result with the formula shown on the page.

What formula does the Fund Comparison Calculator use?

It calculates Net return = Expected return - Expense ratio and Future value = Investment x (1 + Net return) ^ Years. The tool reports fund a value, fund b value, difference, and better net return.

Where do I get the numbers for the Fund Comparison Calculator?

Take investment amount, fund a return (%), fund a expense (%), fund b return (%), fund b expense (%), and years from your fund statement or scheme factsheet. The Fund Comparison Calculator never fetches live market data, so the output depends only on the values you type in.

Are the Fund Comparison Calculator results financial advice?

No. The Fund Comparison Calculator is an educational tool. Treat fund a value as an estimate, re-check your inputs, and confirm important investment decisions with a qualified professional.

Can I use the Fund Comparison Calculator with any currency or market?

Yes. Keep investment amount, fund a return (%), fund a expense (%), fund b return (%), fund b expense (%), and years in the same currency and consistent units, and the Fund Comparison Calculator applies the same calculation to any market.
