Frequently asked questions

What is the Fund Overlap Calculator used for?

The Fund Overlap Calculator is a free mutual funds calculator that works out overlap vs fund a from common holdings value, fund a holdings value, and fund b holdings value. Enter your own figures to get an instant result with the formula shown on the page.

What formula does the Fund Overlap Calculator use?

It calculates Overlap = Common holdings / Fund holdings x 100. The tool reports overlap vs fund a, overlap vs fund b, common holdings, and average overlap.

Where do I get the numbers for the Fund Overlap Calculator?

Take common holdings value, fund a holdings value, and fund b holdings value from your fund statement or scheme factsheet. The Fund Overlap Calculator never fetches live market data, so the output depends only on the values you type in.

Are the Fund Overlap Calculator results financial advice?

No. The Fund Overlap Calculator is an educational tool. Treat overlap vs fund a as an estimate, re-check your inputs, and confirm important investment decisions with a qualified professional.

Can I use the Fund Overlap Calculator with any currency or market?

Yes. Keep common holdings value, fund a holdings value, and fund b holdings value in the same currency and consistent units, and the Fund Overlap Calculator applies the same calculation to any market.
