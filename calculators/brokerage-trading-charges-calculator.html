FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is the Brokerage & Trading Charges Calculator used for?

The Brokerage & Trading Charges Calculator is a free stocks calculator that works out total charges from buy turnover, sell turnover, brokerage, exchange/transaction charges, taxes and regulatory fees, and other charges. Enter your own figures to get an instant result with the formula shown on the page.

What formula does the Brokerage & Trading Charges Calculator use?

It calculates Total turnover = Buy turnover + Sell turnover, Total charges = Brokerage + Exchange charges + Taxes + Other charges, and Charge rate = Total charges / Total turnover x 100. The tool reports total charges, total turnover, charge rate, and net proceeds after charges.

Where do I get the numbers for the Brokerage & Trading Charges Calculator?

Take buy turnover, sell turnover, brokerage, exchange/transaction charges, taxes and regulatory fees, and other charges from your broker statement or exchange. The Brokerage & Trading Charges Calculator never fetches live market data, so the output depends only on the values you type in.

Are the Brokerage & Trading Charges Calculator results financial advice?

No. The Brokerage & Trading Charges Calculator is an educational tool. Treat total charges as an estimate, re-check your inputs, and confirm important investment decisions with a qualified professional.

Can I use the Brokerage & Trading Charges Calculator with any currency or market?

Yes. Keep buy turnover, sell turnover, brokerage, exchange/transaction charges, taxes and regulatory fees, and other charges in the same currency and consistent units, and the Brokerage & Trading Charges Calculator applies the same calculation to any market.
