When to use this calculator

Use this calculator before or after a stock trade to estimate net profit or loss after buy price, sell price, share quantity, and charges. It is useful for comparing trade outcomes and understanding how brokerage or fees change your break-even point.

How to interpret the result

A positive result means the trade is profitable before any tax assumptions. ROI shows the return relative to total cost, while break-even sell price shows the minimum price needed to recover cost and charges.

Common mistakes to avoid

Do not mix currencies or units. Include both buy-side and sell-side charges when available. If tax applies in your country, use after-tax calculators or verify with a qualified tax professional.

The Stock Profit / Loss Calculator helps you calculate net Profit / Loss from buy price per share, sell price per share, number of shares, exchange, and brokerage (Buy + Sell), with instant result cards and a clear formula reference.
