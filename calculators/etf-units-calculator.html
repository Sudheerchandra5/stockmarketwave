What this calculator helps with

The ETF Units Calculator estimates units from investment amount and NAV / price. It is a focused ETF calculator you can use free with no signup, repeating the calculation with your own numbers whenever your inputs change.

How to read the results

After you press Calculate result, the summary cards show units, investment amount, NAV / price, and value per 100 units. Read units as the headline figure and use the supporting values for context, then change one input at a time to see how sensitive the result is.

Before relying on the numbers

This ETF calculator applies the formula Investment amount / NAV. Enter investment amount and NAV / price using consistent units and the same currency, include any applicable fees or taxes, and confirm important decisions against your broker, fund statement, exchange data, or a qualified professional.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is the ETF Units Calculator used for?

The ETF Units Calculator is a free etfs calculator that works out units from investment amount and nav / price. Enter your own figures to get an instant result with the formula shown on the page.

What formula does the ETF Units Calculator use?

It calculates Units = Investment amount / NAV. The tool reports units, investment amount, nav / price, and value per 100 units.

Where do I get the numbers for the ETF Units Calculator?

Take investment amount and nav / price from your ETF provider page or exchange. The ETF Units Calculator never fetches live market data, so the output depends only on the values you type in.

Are the ETF Units Calculator results financial advice?

No. The ETF Units Calculator is an educational tool. Treat units as an estimate, re-check your inputs, and confirm important investment decisions with a qualified professional.

Can I use the ETF Units Calculator with any currency or market?

Yes. Keep investment amount and nav / price in the same currency and consistent units, and the ETF Units Calculator applies the same calculation to any market.
