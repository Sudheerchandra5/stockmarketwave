When to use this calculator

Use this ETF profit/loss calculator to estimate net result from buying and selling ETF units after fees. It works for equity ETFs, bond ETFs, international ETFs, and other exchange-traded funds.

How to interpret the result

Profit or loss depends on price movement, units, and trading fees. ROI compares the net result against the initial ETF purchase value.

Common mistakes to avoid

Remember that ETF execution can include bid-ask spread, brokerage, taxes, and currency impact for international ETFs. Include these costs where relevant.
