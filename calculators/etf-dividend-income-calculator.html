What this calculator helps with

The ETF Dividend Income Calculator estimates annual income from units / shares, payout per unit, and payments per year. It is a focused ETF calculator you can use free with no signup, repeating the calculation with your own numbers whenever your inputs change.

How to read the results

After you press Calculate result, the summary cards show annual income, income per payout, units / shares, and payments per year. Read annual income as the headline figure and use the supporting values for context, then change one input at a time to see how sensitive the result is.

Before relying on the numbers

This ETF calculator applies the formula Units x Payout per unit x Payments per year. Enter units / shares, payout per unit, and payments per year using consistent units and the same currency, include any applicable fees or taxes, and confirm important decisions against your broker, fund statement, exchange data, or a qualified professional.
