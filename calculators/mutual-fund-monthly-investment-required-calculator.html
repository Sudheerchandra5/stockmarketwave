What is the Monthly Investment Required Calculator used for?

The Monthly Investment Required Calculator is a free mutual funds calculator that works out required monthly investment from goal amount, expected annual return (%), and years. Enter your own figures to get an instant result with the formula shown on the page.

What formula does the Monthly Investment Required Calculator use?

It calculates Required monthly investment = Goal amount / SIP future value factor. The tool reports required monthly investment, goal amount, months, and expected return.

Where do I get the numbers for the Monthly Investment Required Calculator?

Take goal amount, expected annual return (%), and years from your fund statement or scheme factsheet. The Monthly Investment Required Calculator never fetches live market data, so the output depends only on the values you type in.

Are the Monthly Investment Required Calculator results financial advice?

No. The Monthly Investment Required Calculator is an educational tool. Treat required monthly investment as an estimate, re-check your inputs, and confirm important investment decisions with a qualified professional.

Can I use the Monthly Investment Required Calculator with any currency or market?

Yes. Keep goal amount, expected annual return (%), and years in the same currency and consistent units, and the Monthly Investment Required Calculator applies the same calculation to any market.
