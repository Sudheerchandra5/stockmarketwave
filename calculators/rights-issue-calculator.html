FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is the Rights Issue Calculator used for?

The Rights Issue Calculator is a free stocks calculator that works out rights shares from existing shares, market price, rights shares offered, for every shares held, and rights issue price. Enter your own figures to get an instant result with the formula shown on the page.

What formula does the Rights Issue Calculator use?

It calculates Rights shares = Existing shares x Rights offered / Shares held and TERP = (Current value + Rights cost) / Total shares. The tool reports rights shares, rights cost, total shares after rights, and theoretical ex-rights price.

Where do I get the numbers for the Rights Issue Calculator?

Take existing shares, market price, rights shares offered, for every shares held, and rights issue price from your broker statement or exchange. The Rights Issue Calculator never fetches live market data, so the output depends only on the values you type in.

Are the Rights Issue Calculator results financial advice?

No. The Rights Issue Calculator is an educational tool. Treat rights shares as an estimate, re-check your inputs, and confirm important investment decisions with a qualified professional.

Can I use the Rights Issue Calculator with any currency or market?

Yes. Keep existing shares, market price, rights shares offered, for every shares held, and rights issue price in the same currency and consistent units, and the Rights Issue Calculator applies the same calculation to any market.
