Frequently asked questions

What is the ETF vs Index Fund Calculator used for?

The ETF vs Index Fund Calculator is a free etfs calculator that works out etf value from investment amount, etf return (%), etf cost (%), index fund return (%), index fund cost (%), and years. Enter your own figures to get an instant result with the formula shown on the page.

What formula does the ETF vs Index Fund Calculator use?

It calculates Net return = Gross return - Cost and Future value = Investment x (1 + Net return) ^ Years. The tool reports etf value, index fund value, difference, and net return gap.

Where do I get the numbers for the ETF vs Index Fund Calculator?

Take investment amount, etf return (%), etf cost (%), index fund return (%), index fund cost (%), and years from your ETF provider page or exchange. The ETF vs Index Fund Calculator never fetches live market data, so the output depends only on the values you type in.

Are the ETF vs Index Fund Calculator results financial advice?

No. The ETF vs Index Fund Calculator is an educational tool. Treat etf value as an estimate, re-check your inputs, and confirm important investment decisions with a qualified professional.

Can I use the ETF vs Index Fund Calculator with any currency or market?

Yes. Keep investment amount, etf return (%), etf cost (%), index fund return (%), index fund cost (%), and years in the same currency and consistent units, and the ETF vs Index Fund Calculator applies the same calculation to any market.
